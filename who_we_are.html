Who We Are

NEYA is a non-profit Organization that focuses on youth development and education in the New England Area.

Mission Statement

NEYA’s purpose is to provide a positive, fun, and safe environment for American Muslim Youth in the New England area. The organization’s goals are to empower youth to be model citizens, and to contribute to their personal and social development through rich educational, athletic, and social activities designed to develop skills, build confidence, foster teamwork, promote self-esteem, and cultivate character and leadership.
